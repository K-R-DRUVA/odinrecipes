Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta with the garlic in olive oil until crispy. Remove garlic after cooking.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with Pecorino and Parmesan cheeses.
  4. Drain the pasta, then quickly mix it with the pancetta and egg mixture while still hot.
  5. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
