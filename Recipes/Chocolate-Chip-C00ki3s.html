Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  3. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually stir into the creamed mixture. Mix in chocolate chips.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until edges are golden.
